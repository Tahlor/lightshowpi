Control with Google Assistant

Use the following voice commands with Google Assistant to control your LightShowPi:

  • "Activate Christmas tree" - Turns on the lights.
  • "Activate Christmas tree off" - Turns off the lights.
  • "Activate light show" - Enables microphone-controlled power levels.

Webhook Integration

To integrate with other services, use the following webhook:

https://taylorarchibald.com/lightshowpi

Send data as application/x-www-form-urlencoded with the key message=Start where Start is your command.
